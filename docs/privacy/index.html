Receta Bebesh Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 29, 2026

Receta Bebesh provides meal planning, baby recipe discovery, shopping lists, and feeding-related support for parents and caregivers. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to users.

Information we collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, and authentication details.
  • Profile information such as baby name, baby birthdate, selected language, and optional profile photo.
  • App content created by the user, including favorites, meal plans, shopping lists, notes, ratings, and tracker entries.
  • Notification preferences and reminder settings stored to support app functionality.
  • Information sent when AI meal-planning features are used, such as baby age stage and recipe selections required to generate a plan.

App Store privacy summary

Based on the current version of Receta Bebesh, the app may collect data that is linked to the user to provide account, planning, personalization, and support features.

  • Contact Info: name and email address.
  • User Content: profile photo, favorites, meal plans, shopping lists, notes, ratings, and related entries.
  • Sensitive or Personal Information entered by the user: baby name, baby birthdate, feeding-related entries, and growth-tracker data.

Receta Bebesh does not use collected data for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

How we use information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To personalize recipes, meal plans, and baby-related content.
  • To save app data across devices and sessions.
  • To provide reminder notifications when enabled by the user.
  • To improve app stability, resolve support issues, and maintain the service.

Storage and services

Receta Bebesh uses third-party infrastructure such as Firebase to store account and app data. AI planning features may use Google Gemini services when that feature is enabled.

Photos and permissions

If you choose to upload a profile photo, the app requests access to your photo library. Photos are used only for your profile inside the app.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app may schedule local notifications on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in device settings.

Authentication

You may sign in with email and password or supported sign-in providers such as Google, depending on platform configuration. Authentication data is used only to create and secure your account.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only with service providers that help operate the app, or when required by law.

Data retention

We retain account and app data for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your choices

  • You can update profile information in the app.
  • You can stop using optional features such as profile photos and notifications.
  • You can contact us to request account or data deletion.

Data deletion requests

To request deletion of your account or stored data, contact goriandriza11@gmail.com from the email associated with your account.

Children's privacy

Receta Bebesh is intended for parents and caregivers. It is not directed to children to use independently.

Contact
